Act Report

¶

In this project, we wrangled, analysed and visualised the dataset of the tweet archive of Twitter user @dog_rates, also known as WeRateDogs. WeRateDogs is a Twitter account that rates people's dogs with a humorous comment about the dog.

First of all, 3 datasets have been gathered to perform a better analysis, The WeRateDogs Twitter archive, The tweet image predictions and Additional data from the Twitter API.
For the assessment part, the two types have been required. For visual assessment, I used Sublime and Google Sheets, along with the non directed visuel assessment through juputer notebook to detect pertinent issues. For programmatic assessment, I used pandas Methods and functions to perform directed and non directed methods assessment. I detected 8 quality issues and two tidinrss issues.
For the cleaning part, I used the define, code, and test steps of the cleaning process. I utilized the most common cleaning functions and methods in the pandas library to clean the eight quality issues and two tidiness issues identified in the previous step Assessing Data.
After the gatherin, assessing and cleaning processes, I had a tidy and high quality data.\

Then, I performed the analysis and the visualization and generated a few insights.

Correlation between favorite count and retweet count¶

For the following chart, I tried to identify if there is a correlation between the favorite count and the retweet count.

The above scatter plot shows a positive correlation between the favorite count and the retweet count. Which means that the more the gog is liked, the more the tweet will be retweeted and vice versa.

Favorite dog stages per rating¶

Here, I wanted to categorise the dog stages per their rating numerator and to identify which dog stage with a specific rating have been liked the most.

It can be seen from the above bar plot, that the most rated dog stage is the doggo stage with the rating numerator of 13 with 537K favorite counts. Followed by, the pupper dog stage with the rating numerator of 12 and a favorite count of 450K.

Top retweeted dogs names per rating¶

For this last part, I wanted to identify the dog with the higher retweeted count, and to know its rating and name.

As shown from the chart above, the most retweeted dog is BO with 42.2K retweets and a rating of 14. Followed by, Jamesy with 32.9K retweets and a rating of 13.
